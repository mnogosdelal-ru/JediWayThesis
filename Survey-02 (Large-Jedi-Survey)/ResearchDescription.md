# Research: Effectiveness of Popular Productivity Techniques

## Research Question
To what extent are productivity techniques from popular science literature actually effective based on scientific evidence?

## Research Objectives
1. Identify commonly recommended productivity techniques from popular literature
2. Evaluate the scientific evidence supporting each technique
3. Measure practical effectiveness through surveys/experiments
4. Compare perceived vs. actual effectiveness

## Methodology

### Phase 1: Literature Review
- [ ] Search academic databases (Google Scholar, PubMed, PsycINFO)
- [ ] Identify peer-reviewed studies for each technique
- [ ] Rate evidence quality (sample size, methodology, reproducibility)

### Phase 2: Survey Design
- [ ] Create participant screening questionnaire
- [ ] Design technique adoption survey
- [ ] Develop effectiveness rating scales
- [ ] Set up control variables (age, profession, baseline productivity)

### Phase 3: Data Collection

### Phase 4: Analysis
- [ ] Statistical analysis of effectiveness ratings
